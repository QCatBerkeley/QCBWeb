Qubit Project

The qubit project aims to construct simulations of low-qubit quantum computers on classical computers. This is accomplished by first constructing simulations of qubits, then simulating increasingly complex gate operations on them. The ultimate goal is to implement both Shor's and Grover's algorithms on the resulting, simulated quantum computer.
